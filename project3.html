and Manage Inspirations

MY ROLE
UI/UX Designer
PROJECT TYPE
Individual Project
TIMELINE
15 Weeks
Problem Statement
  • Pain Point

    As a Design student, I need to gather inspirations from all the sourses that I attached everyday. There are a lot of apps I download in my phone for saving inspiration. Each app have folders inside. It’s inconvenient to manage them by switching different apps.

  • Goal

    My goal is to create an app that can gather all the app for catch inspirations together. To biuld an esay way for managing all the folders from different apps. And also a community that people can share interesting ideas and works to others and make friends.

    • Design Process

    Research
    I interviewed four persons from different major to know better about how people gather inspirations and manage them in thier daily life to better understand these learning goals.
    Learning Goal 1 To understand what habits people have when they are searching for inspirations online.
    Learning Goal 2 To understand how people save thier inspirations in the daily life.
    Competitors Analysis
    Pinterest
    Adventages:

    1. Pinterest have tags for classifing the pinstures and folders.

    2. The layout of Pinterest is very clear to show more pictures.

    Disadventages:

    Some app or website needs too many steps for saving pictures, like Pinterest. It’s hard to manage and move pictures to diferrent folders.

    Sitemap
    Based on the reserch result I did before, I list down some ideas for the app design. I use Miro to create a mindmap board that helps me to think which functions I should add and what forms of inspiration I will make in my app design. It makes the navigation of my app design more clear.
    From the sitemap, I made a list of main features that my app will have are as bellow:
    Save inspirations of different forms

    Create a new work of variable forms, like photo editing, add text and stickers, drawing, and picture collage, etc. Save them to the local folders.

    Community for sharing works and make friends

    Post works for sharing to other people and get comments. View other people’s works to get new ideas. Also follow the people you like and make friends.

    Manage folders of other apps

    By link to other app, you can manage all the folders easier through using only one app.

    Personas
    To understand better of how people will use my app, I create two personas that are from the target users of my app. Based on each background, I made user flows for each person and create my wireframes from that.

    User Test

    Two Rounds of User Test
    I did two rounds of user test. I interviewed 6 people from students to work people. They are from different majors and occupations. I let them test the user flow to know better how the real users will use my app. Is it easy to use? Are there any confusing steps? And how they feel about my design. I got lots of feedback from them, which helps me a lot for the futher modifying.
    A/B Test
    I also Design a A/B test version of feature two. To learn which design page layout converts into higher usage. The metric they are tracking would be how long time they finish the flow.
