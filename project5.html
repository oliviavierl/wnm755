International Smoke Redesign
I rebranded a San Francisco local bakery called 'Zanze’s Cheesecake' by designing new logos, brand collateral, interactive menus and website.
International Smoke is the wildly popular collaboration between Chef Michael Mina and celebrity entrepreneur, television host, and New York Times best-selling author Ayesha Curry. Inspired by Curry and Mina’s shared love of global travels and international cooking techniques, International Smoke ignites a newspark of avor and creativity focused on woodred steaks, seafood, ribs, and vegetarian entrees to create flavorful dishes that are craveable worldwide.
CURRENT SITUATION
Logo
The present logo is an complex illustration. The shape looks like a bull. It’s hard to associate with the brand. And it’s also not easy for customers to remember.
Website

Menu
